# Battle Card — Clinisys × Lab Director

> Competitor: [[clinisys]] | Persona: [[persona-lab-director]]

Prospect runs Clinisys (or Sunquest/Horizon legacy), writing to **Lab Director / Manager**.

## Setup

**Prospect signals**: same as `clinisys_x_ceo-owner.md`.
**Persona signals**: Lab Director, Lab Manager, Director of Operations.

## Lead with these pains

1. **Legacy UX**: Sunquest/Horizon UX is decades old; daily friction adds up
2. **Multi-day update windows** — on-prem upgrades require weekend maintenance
3. **Reporting flexibility** — limited compared to modern tools
4. **Historical data continuity concerns** during any migration

## Quote pool (≤15 words each)

After `lims-pain-extractor` runs, replace with real:

- _"Daily workflows feel slower than they should — UX is dated."_ — paraphrase scaffold
- _"Upgrade weekends still happen. Cloud peers don't have those."_ — paraphrase scaffold

## CTA

- ✅ "Want to see how we preserve 15 years of Sunquest result history queryable post-migration?"
- ✅ "Curious how mirror phase looks for a Sunquest → Vivica transition specifically?"

## Objections to expect

| Objection | Response |
|---|---|
| "We have 15 years of result history in Sunquest." | "We import full historical results in mirror phase — searchable, exportable, audit-traceable. You don't lose the past; you stop adding to a legacy stack." |
| "Our team knows Sunquest workflows by heart." | "Mirror phase replicates your current workflows in Vivica. Day 1 looks the same to your team. Modernizations happen incrementally, with team buy-in." |
| "Upgrades are scary on cloud — no control." | "Cloud means no upgrades for your team. We push features continuously; nothing breaks because every change goes through the same automated test suite Acure relies on. No more weekend windows." |
