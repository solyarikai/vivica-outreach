## Market position

CloudLIMS is a direct competitor on the **"low IT burden, low upfront cost"** axis. Multi-vertical product (clinical is one of several markets). Published pricing tiers, fast trial-to-production path. Resembles Vivica in go-to-market shape, less so in clinical depth.

Sales motion: self-serve trial → inside sales. Less enterprise procurement than Clinisys/LabWare.

## Where Vivica wins

- **Clinical depth**: anatomic pathology, complex molecular reporting, narrative interpretation — CloudLIMS is broader and shallower in clinical
- **Reference-lab routing**: complex client-routing patterns specific to clinical reference labs
- **HIPAA / compliance posture** for clinical-only customers vs CloudLIMS's multi-vertical compliance surface
- **US-team alignment** for US-based clinical workflows

## Where CloudLIMS wins

- **Multi-vertical operations** that span clinical + non-clinical use cases
- **Self-serve trial** with no sales contact required
- **Generic LIMS capabilities** for labs with simpler workflows

## Migration angle

Three-phase framework applies. CloudLIMS exports are SaaS-friendly (CSV / JSON / API). Mirror phase is technically smooth.

The interesting migration conversation: **why is the customer leaving CloudLIMS for Vivica?** Usually it's "we outgrew their clinical depth" — Vivica leans into that with concrete examples.
