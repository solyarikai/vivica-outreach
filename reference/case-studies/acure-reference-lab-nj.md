## Why Acure matters for outreach

Three things make this reference unusually strong:

1. **Reference Lab tier** — Reference Labs are the highest-trust tier of the lab pyramid. Other labs send their hardest specimens here. If Acure picked Vivica, Vivica is good enough for the labs that judge other labs.

2. **$100M+ revenue** — this is not a startup running a side project. This is a serious operating business with serious compliance and operational stakes.

3. **Joint published research with KOL** — Acure and Vivica co-authored a study with a recognized Key Opinion Leader. **Public, citable, name-droppable in cold emails.** ✓ confirm exact title + journal + KOL name with Chris before quoting in copy.
