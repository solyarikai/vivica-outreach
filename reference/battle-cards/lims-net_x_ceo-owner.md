## Setup

**Prospect signals** (from `lims-detector`):
- Patient portal iframe matches `*.lims.net` or `*.limseo.eu`
- French-language artifacts in their portal or documentation

**Special notes**:
- High overlap with Russian-speaking NJ segment per Petr's chat. **Always check russian-segment status first** — if russian-speaking owner, route to Andrew (`../playbooks/russian-segment.md`).
- French-origin product → US-market gaps story is the right angle

## Lead with these pains

1. **US-market coverage gaps** — features and integrations don't always match US clinical needs
2. **EU-primary support** — time-zone misalignment for US business hours
3. **Translation overhead** — interfaces and docs feel translated rather than US-native
4. **Growth ceiling** — labs outgrow what the platform does in the US clinical market

## Quote pool (≤15 words each)

- _"Documentation occasionally has French artifacts."_ — paraphrase scaffold
