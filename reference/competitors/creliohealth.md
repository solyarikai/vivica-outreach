## Market position

Indian-headquartered combined LIS+LIMS with a foothold in US mid-market diagnostic labs. Transparent tiered pricing (starter / professional / enterprise) makes them attractive to budget-conscious labs. Multi-country product means feature priorities split across regions.

Sales motion: inside sales, demo-driven, fast onboarding for small labs.

## Where Vivica wins

- **US-deployment depth**: Vivica is US-built for US workflows (CLIA/CAP/HIPAA primary), CrelioHealth covers many regions.
- **US support hours**: time-zone alignment with US labs.
- **Anatomic pathology depth** for reference-lab use cases.
- **Reference-lab routing and complex test menus**.

## Where CrelioHealth wins

- **Price point** at low tiers (starter/professional)
- **Fast onboarding** for small POL/PSC labs
- **International reach** if a customer has labs across regions
